How I Learned to Stop Procrastinating, & Love Letting Go

Sun Mar 18, 2018 by Timani Tunduwani in  Procrastinating

The end of procrastination is the art of letting go. I’ve been a lifelong procrastinator, at least until recent years. I would put things off until deadline, because I knew I could come through. I came through on tests after cramming last minute, I turned articles in at the deadline after waiting until the last hour, I got things done. Until I didn’t.

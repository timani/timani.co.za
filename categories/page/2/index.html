Fearlessness: How to Stop Running from Space

Sun Mar 18, 2018 by Timani Tunduwani in  Space

We spend our days filling in every available space, cramming in more tasks, responding to messages, checking social media and online sites, watching videos. We are afraid of empty space in our lives. The result is often a continual busyness, constant distraction and avoidance, lack of focus, lack of satisfaction with our lives. We run from silence.
